Pig Feed and Range

  1. Pig Creep meal

    2. This is a high protein diet which is highly digestible and palatable and is designed to stimulate feeding in new ly fostered pigs from about seven days of age up to a period of about 35 days

    Piglets are ussaully weaned from between 20 to 30 days after farrowing and this creep meal can be fed up to a week after weaning

  2. Pig Weaner 1 Meal

    3. This is a highly digestible and highly palatable feed designed to address the nutritional needs for newly weaned piglets with slightly higher energy and slightly lower crude protein content compared to the creep meal

  3. Pig Weaner 2 Meal

    4. This is a highly digestible and palatable feed specially designed with a higher energy to meet the rapid growth demands of weaner piglets

  4. Pig Grower Meal

    5. This is a grower with a crude protein percentage of 16% and an energy value of 13.2MJ/kg.This feed is designed for growing pigs and is fed for a period of 56 days after the Weaner 2 meal
